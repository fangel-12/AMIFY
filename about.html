About Amify

Helping Nigerian farmers build trust through digital farm records.

Amify is a modern farm management platform designed to help farmers keep accurate records, improve productivity, and earn buyer confidence. Every harvest, expense, treatment, and activity is stored in one secure place.

Our Mission

To make digital farm record keeping simple, affordable, and accessible for every farmer in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted agricultural technology platform, connecting farmers with opportunities through transparent records.

Why Amify?

Many farmers still rely on notebooks or memory to manage their farms. Important information gets lost, making it difficult to track profits, monitor crops, or prove farming history to buyers and partners.

Amify solves this by providing a simple digital platform where every farm activity is recorded and organized. Farmers gain better control of their operations while buyers can make decisions with greater confidence.
