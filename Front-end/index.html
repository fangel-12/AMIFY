Why We Exist

Trust shouldn't be a barrier to doing business.

Every season, hardworking farmers produce quality crops but struggle to prove their reliability to new buyers. At the same time, buyers find it difficult to know which farmers they can trust.

We created Amify to bridge that gap. By helping farmers build a verified history of their farming activities, we're making it easier for buyers to find trusted suppliers and for honest farmers to earn more opportunities.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Amify free for farmers?+
Yes — signing up, logging records, and building your reliability score is free for farmers, always.
How does verification work?+
We confirm your phone number and a photo of your farm to give you a Verified badge, this builds buyer trust before you even message them.
Do I have to pay to find buyers?+
No — browsing and chatting is free. We only take a small fee on deals completed and confirmed through the app.
What crops can I list?+
Maize, cassava, yam, rice, tomato, pepper, beans, and more, you're not limited to one crop.
